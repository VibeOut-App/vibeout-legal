Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 5, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information that you provide directly:

  • Account information (your name and email address, collected during sign-up)
  • Location data (your approximate location, only while the app is open and only when you grant permission)

We also collect the following usage data automatically:

  • Interaction data — when you tap to view a menu, make a reservation, call a venue, or get directions, we record the type of action and which venue it was for
  • Check-in data — when you use the "I'm Here" feature, we record your proximity to the venue to verify your visit

Your account information is stored securely in Google Firebase. We do not collect crash reports or device-level telemetry beyond what is described above.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Authenticate your account and keep you signed in
  • Display your name and email on your profile screen
  • Show happy hour venues on a map relative to your location
  • Track your interactions with venue listings (directions, calls, menu views, and reservation taps) to provide venue owners with aggregated, anonymous engagement analytics
  • Verify your location when you check in at a venue
  • Improve and maintain the app

3. Location Data

VibeOut requests access to your location only while the app is in use ("When In Use" permission). Your location is used to:

  • Center the map on your position and display nearby happy hours
  • Verify your proximity to a venue when you use the "I'm Here" check-in feature

When you check in, a single location reading is taken to confirm you are within approximately 200 meters of the venue. The distance (not your exact coordinates) is stored alongside the check-in record.

Your location is never tracked in the background. If you deny location permission, the app will still function using a default map location, but the check-in feature will be unavailable. You can change your location permission at any time in your device's Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication (Google) — to manage your account sign-in securely
  • Cloud Firestore (Google) — to store your account profile and retrieve happy hour listings
  • Apple MapKit — to display the map and venue locations within the app
  • Google AdMob — to display advertisements within the app. AdMob may collect your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) and other device information to serve relevant ads. You can limit ad tracking at any time via Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking on your device.

When you choose to navigate to a venue, the app may open Apple Maps or Google Maps. These apps are governed by their own privacy policies.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your account data (name and email) for as long as your account is active. Interaction and check-in records are retained to provide venue owners with analytics; these records are associated with your account ID but are only shared with venue owners in aggregated, anonymous form.

When you delete your account, your profile data is permanently removed. You can also request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at vibeoutapp@outlook.com.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you (your name and email)
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data, including interaction and check-in records
  • Revoke location permission at any time via your device settings

7. Data Security

Your data is transmitted over encrypted connections (TLS/SSL) and stored in Google Firebase, which provides industry-standard security protections. Authentication tokens are managed securely by the Firebase SDK. On a fresh app install, any stale credentials from a previous installation are automatically cleared.

8. Age Requirement

VibeOut is intended for users who are of legal drinking age (21 years or older in the United States). We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 21. If we learn that we have collected data from a user under 21, we will delete their account promptly.

9. Advertising

VibeOut is free to use and is supported by advertising through Google AdMob. Ads are displayed as small banners within the app. Google AdMob may use your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) and general location to show you ads that are relevant to your interests.

You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time by:

  • Going to Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking and disabling tracking for VibeOut
  • Going to Settings > Privacy & Security > Apple Advertising and disabling Personalized Ads

Opting out means you will still see ads, but they will not be personalized based on your interests. For more information on how Google uses data from apps that use AdMob, visit google.com/policies/privacy/partners.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the updated policy in the app and revising the "Last Updated" date above.
