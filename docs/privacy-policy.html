Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 16, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information that you provide directly:

  • Account information (your name, email address, and business name, collected during sign-up)
  • Happy hour listing data (venue name, address, coordinates, hours, deal descriptions, menu links, and active days)

We do not collect analytics, crash reports, or device-level usage data beyond what is necessary to operate the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Authenticate your account and verify your owner status
  • Display your name, email, and business name on your profile screen
  • Publish your happy hour listings so they are visible to VibeOut consumer app users
  • Allow you to create, edit, and delete your listings
  • Provide you with aggregated, anonymous analytics about how users interact with your listings (e.g., direction taps, menu views, calls, reservation taps, and verified check-ins)
  • Improve and maintain the app

3. Business Information Visibility

Happy hour listings you create are publicly visible to all authenticated VibeOut users. This includes:

  • Venue name and address
  • Happy hour times and active days
  • Deal and special descriptions
  • Menu link (if provided)
  • Venue location on the map (latitude and longitude)

Your personal account information (your name and email) is not shared publicly and is only visible to you on your profile screen.

4. Analytics Data

The Analytics tab in the app provides aggregated engagement metrics for your happy hour listings. This data is derived from interactions by VibeOut consumer app users and includes:

  • Direction taps, phone calls, menu views, and reservation taps
  • Verified check-in counts (when a consumer uses the "I'm Here" feature near your venue)

All analytics are presented as aggregate counts. You do not receive any personally identifiable information about individual VibeOut users. Analytics data is collected from consumer app users who have agreed to VibeOut's consumer Privacy Policy and Terms of Use.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication (Google) — to manage your account sign-in securely
  • Cloud Firestore (Google) — to store your account profile and happy hour listings

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your account data and happy hour listings for as long as your account is active. When you delete a listing through the app, it is permanently removed from our database and will no longer appear to VibeOut users.

You can request deletion of your entire account and all associated data (including all listings) by contacting us at vibeoutapp@outlook.com.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Create, edit, or remove your happy hour listings at any time through the app

8. Data Security

Your data is transmitted over encrypted connections (TLS/SSL) and stored in Google Firebase, which provides industry-standard security protections. Firestore security rules ensure that only you can read and modify your owner profile, and only you can edit or delete your own listings. Authentication tokens are managed securely by the Firebase SDK.

9. Age Requirement

VibeOut is intended for users who are of legal drinking age (21 years or older in the United States). We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 21.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the updated policy in the app and revising the "Last Updated" date above.
